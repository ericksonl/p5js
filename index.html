How To Use:

Experience the creative process with this program that generates dynamic spheres to paint the canvas! Click the "start" button and customize various parameters to achieve your desired result.

Opacity

Adjust the transparency of the spawned spheres to create different visual effects.

Ball Size

Modify the size of the spheres to influence the overall appearance of the artwork.

Scatter

Determine the spread or scatter between each sphere. Higher scatter values result in spheres being painted farther apart, while lower values bring them closer together.

Generations

Control the number of generations a spawned ball receives. More generations mean the sphere will paint for a longer duration.
